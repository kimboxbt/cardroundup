Affiliate Policy

Last updated: March 2026

How We Make Money

CardRoundup is free to use. Some of the "Apply" buttons on the site are affiliate links — if you click one and are approved for the card, we may receive a commission from the card issuer. This comes at no cost to you; the issuer pays the commission, not you.

What Doesn't Change

Affiliate relationships have zero influence on our data or editorial decisions. Every card in our database is there because it's a card people actually carry, not because of an affiliate relationship. Benefit data is sourced from issuer benefit pages, The Points Guy, NerdWallet, and Doctor of Credit — and checked weekly for accuracy.

Cards without affiliate links are treated identically to cards with them. We do not rank, sort, highlight, or recommend cards based on commission rates.

FTC Disclosure

In accordance with FTC guidelines, we disclose that some links on CardRoundup are affiliate links. The footer on every page includes a notice of this relationship.
